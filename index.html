Education

2021-Currently PhD: Currently, I'm studying my PhD in Software Engineering in University of Mons (UMONS). Under the supervision of Tom Mens, I continue to study collaborative software development platforms specially GitHub and solve the issue related to usage of CI/CD tools in them.

2019-2021 Master Degree: I hold a master degree in Software Engineering from Sharif University of Technology (SUT), supervised by Dr. Abbas Heydarnoori. I studied collaborative software development platforms and tried to solve the issues exists within them. Our research with my co-supervisor Maliheh Izadi resulted an ICSME 2021 paper on automated recovery of issue-commit links for further usage in software evolution.

2015-2019 Bachelor Degree: I was a bachelor student of Computer Science at K. N. Toosi University of Technology, Tehran, Iran. As for my bachelor thesis, it was about “Deaf Disease Genes Detection Using Artificial Intelligence Tools”. My research was supervised by Dr. Ali Ahmadi. I have finished my bachelor while being ranked among top-3 and having Ghalamchi top-student scholarship for second half of my bachelor degree.

2011-2015 High School: For my high school, I was accepted into one of the most prestigious school discipline in Iran called National Organization for Development of Exceptionally Talented (NODET/SAMPAD) students through national entrance-exam.
