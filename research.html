ABOUT ME

I am currently a PhD student and researcher in the Software Engineering Lab at the University of Mons (UMONS). My research is focused on the usage of CI/CD tools in collaborative software development platforms (specifically GitHub and GitHub Actions). I study the usage of such tools to find the problem exist in their processes and solve them by providing tools to better and easier usage of them in the body of projects.

Research Interests

  • Mining Software Repositories
  • Software Evolution
  • Software Analytics
  • Data Mining
